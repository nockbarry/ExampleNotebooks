Neural Network Model Performance Across Datasets

This project compares the efficiency of various neural network architectures on multiple datasets. Models like `16_16_3`, `32_32_3`, and `64_64_3` refer to architectures with two hidden layers of respective neuron counts and a 3-neuron output layer for classification, operating on a 2-dimensional input shape.

For each dataset, an animated visualization captures the decision boundary evolution during training at intervals of every 10 epochs.

Terms:
- Training Loss: The error metric during model training. Lower values suggest a better fit.
- Accuracy: The ratio of correct predictions by the model.
- Validation Loss and Accuracy: Metrics on a separate data subset, indicating performance on unseen data.
- Epochs: Complete passes through the entire training dataset during model training.
